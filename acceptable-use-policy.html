ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY

Last Updated: May 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy (“AUP”) defines the rules and guidelines for using the JP Kreasi platform and services.

By using our platform, you agree to comply with this policy and ensure that all activities performed through your account follow these guidelines.

1. Purpose of the Platform

JP Kreasi provides tools that help businesses automate and manage customer interactions across digital platforms such as:

  • Facebook Pages
  • Messenger
  • WhatsApp Business

The platform is intended solely for legitimate business communication and customer engagement purposes.

2. Prohibited Activities

Users are strictly prohibited from using the platform for:

  • Sending spam or unsolicited bulk messages
  • Harassment, abuse, or threatening communications
  • Fraudulent or misleading marketing practices
  • Distribution of malware or malicious links
  • Phishing attempts or identity theft
  • Illegal products or services
  • Content that violates intellectual property rights
  • Content that violates Meta platform policies

3. Automation Restrictions

Automation features must be used responsibly.

Users must not:

  • Use automation to generate excessive messaging traffic
  • Send repetitive promotional messages that could be considered spam
  • Automate responses designed to mislead users

JP Kreasi reserves the right to limit or disable automation features if they are used in ways that could harm platform integrity.

4. Compliance with Third-Party Platforms

Because the platform integrates with Meta services, users must also comply with the policies of those platforms, including:

  • Meta Platform Terms
  • Facebook Community Standards
  • Messenger Platform Policies
  • WhatsApp Business Platform Policies

Violations of these policies may result in restrictions imposed by Meta or by JP Kreasi.

5. Security Responsibilities

Users must:

  • Protect account credentials
  • Prevent unauthorized access
  • Immediately report suspected security breaches

Users are responsible for all activity performed through their accounts.

6. Enforcement

JP Kreasi reserves the right to take action if this policy is violated, including:

  • Issuing warnings
  • Temporarily suspending platform access
  • Permanently terminating accounts

Actions will be determined based on the severity of the violation.

7. Policy Updates

This Acceptable Use Policy may be updated periodically.

Updated versions will be published on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
